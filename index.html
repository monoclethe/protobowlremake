How To Play

Play/Pause Button

Starts or pauses the question. Unavailable during buzz.

Skip Button

Skips the current active question. Unavailable while question is not active or during buzz.

Buzz/Submit Button

Buzzes/submits answer. Unavailable while question is not active or paused. Will auto-focus the submission box.

Timer

Counts down your buzz time/time after the question has finished. If the timer finishes, the question is over.

Score

If you get a question wrong before it is finished, you are penalized 5 points and are given a neg. If you finish a question after the power (if there is one), you will be awarded 10 points (correct). If you correctly answer before the power, you will be awarded 15 points (power). Scores are formatted in the following way:
negs/correct/powers
